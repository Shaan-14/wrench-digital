Common Questions

What do I need to provide?
Your business info, logo (if you have one), and any photos. I handle everything else — writing, layout, design, and code.
How do revisions work?
After I deliver a draft, you review and send feedback. We go back and forth until you're happy — within the revision rounds included in your plan.
Do I own the site after?
Yes — 100%. The site, the code, the domain. Everything is yours. You're not locked into anything with me.
Who owns my domain?
You do. Domain management is included in all ongoing plans — I handle registration, DNS, and renewals on your behalf. Your contract guarantees you can take full ownership anytime, no questions asked.
What if I cancel maintenance?
30 days written notice and we're done. I'll transfer your domain to you within 48 hours and your site files are yours to keep.
What if I need something not listed?
Just ask. I'll put together a custom quote for anything outside the standard packages — booking systems, e-commerce setup, custom tools, etc.
